Our Vision

Senior Class Council (SCC) plans some of the most memorable events at Tufts, including Senior Nights, Senior Week and the 100 Days Celebration. Our goal is to plan events and activities that will unite the senior class through unforgettable programming.


SCC's largest event of the year is "The Homecoming Carnival", a football celebration featuring a mechanical shark, popcorn, cotton candy and airbrush tattoos to name a few!


Our most recent "Senior Night" was held at Tequila Rain, on Thursday, November 13th. We hope to see you at the next one!
