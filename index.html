Our Vision

Junior Class Council (JCC) plans social and academic events for both the junior class and the entire Tufts community. We aim to engage as many students as possible, and we do so by offering events that are exciting and appealing to Tufts students.


JCC's largest event of the year is "Fall Gala", a semi-formal event featuring catering, live band, DJ and more. The Gala is a part of "Welcome Weekend", a weekend-long experience featuring live music, great food, photo booths, hypnotists, and fireworks!


Our council has planned "Winter Ball," "Halfway There," "Fall Gala," and more! Look out for our upcoming event, "Junior Sendoff," where we will celebrate world travel, global citizenship and study abroad programs.
