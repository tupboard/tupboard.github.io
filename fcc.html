Our Vision

Freshman Class Council plans social and academic events for the freshman class and the greater Tufts community. The goal of our programming is to unite all freshmen through fun and exciting events which appeal on a broad level.


FCC's largest event of the year is "Tuftonia's Day", an end-of-the-year celebration with countless food-trucks and entertainment, as well as a fireworks show!


We just finished up our competition to design the class banner. Congratulations to our winner, Alex Bass! Stay tuned for more information about when the banner will be up.
