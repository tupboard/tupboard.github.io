Tufts University's Programming Board is a student-run organization that promotes a strong sense of community at Tufts through campus-wide events, activities and social programming. Through its member groups, Programming Board plans both large- and small-scale events tailored to every Tufts student. These member groups include: Freshman Class Council, Sophomore Class Council, Junior Class Council, Senior Class Council, Concert Board, Entertainment Board, and Film Series.

The purpose of Programming Board is to promote a sense of community at Tufts by planning exciting, engaging and enjoyable events for the campus. Our goal is to find innovative and creative ways to unite students at Tufts.
