Our Vision

Sophomore Class Council (SoCC) programs social and academic events for the sophomore class and the greater Tufts community. Our goal is to bring students together by planning exciting events and activities.


One of SoCC's biggest events is "Winter Ball", a semi-formal event in the heart of Boston, held at the beginning of the spring semester. We also plan a halfway there celebration for the Sophomore Class, as well as an Off-Campus Info Session.


This year, "Winter Ball" will be held on Saturday, February 7th 2015. Look out for more information!
