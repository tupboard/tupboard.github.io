About Us

Tufts Film Series has been showing free movies to the Tufts community since 1982. Film Series is made up of Tufts undergraduates who choose, manage and project 35mm films. Film Series want to bring Tufts students together in a relaxed environment, through the love of movies.


In addition to screening weekly movies, Tufts Film Series also holds cosponsorships with other groups on campus like our recent screening of The Rocky Horror Picture Show with Tufts Burlesque and the LGBT Center. Our end of the year event is an outdoor movie on the Residential Quad.


Stay up to date with Tufts Film Series by checking out our website!
